# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

AngularUse is dedicated to providing a welcoming, safe, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Community members are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication
- Assume positive intent and give constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the project and community
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and learn from mistakes

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Repeatedly derailing discussions or disrupting collaboration

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community chat or social channels where the project is represented.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may take action in response to behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Possible actions include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from community spaces, depending on severity and pattern of behavior.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers by opening a private report channel once configured, or by contacting repository owners directly.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.
